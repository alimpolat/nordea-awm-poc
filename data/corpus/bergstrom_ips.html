Bergström Family Office

Client ID: bergstrom · Advisory mandate · Base currency SEK · AUM SEK 480M · Adopted 2025-01-20 · Last reviewed 2026-01-14

1. Objectives & risk profile

The Bergström Family Office holds third-generation industrial wealth (originally pulp & paper, diversified from the 1980s). The mandate is long-term real capital preservation with moderate growth over a rolling 10-year horizon. The family tolerates moderate drawdowns (a single-year decline of up to ~15% is acceptable) in exchange for compounding above Swedish CPI + 3.5% p.a. Liquidity needs are modest; at least 5% of the book is held in instruments realisable within five business days.

3. Restrictions & constraints

  • No direct fossil-fuel producers. No direct holdings in upstream oil, gas, or coal extraction companies. Diversified energy exposure via broad ETFs is permitted.
  • ESG-screened equity. Single-name equity must pass Nordea's ESG screen (no controversial weapons, no UN Global Compact violators).
  • Single-position limit: 5% of total AUM in any single security (funds and broad ETFs exempt).
  • FX policy: a minimum of 60% SEK base-currency exposure across the total book; non-SEK exposure is monitored quarterly.
  • Liquidity floor: ≥5% in instruments realisable within five business days.

4. Stated interests on file

The family has expressed a standing interest in green-bond reallocation within the European fixed-income sleeve, consistent with the ESG screen, to be funded from any rebalancing of overweight sleeves. This is a stated preference, not yet an instruction.

5. Governance

Reviewed at least semi-annually with the lead advisor (Anders Lindqvist). Any change to strategic targets requires sign-off from two family principals. Tactical positioning within bands is delegated to the advisor.
